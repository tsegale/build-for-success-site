Got Questions?
COMMON Q&A
Do I need any experience to join?+
No experience required at all. If you can talk to people and have the drive to earn, you have everything you need. BFS gives you the products and the process — you bring the hustle.
Is there really no registration fee?+
Correct — zero fees to join. We believe in removing barriers. BFS earns when you earn. Our success is tied directly to yours, which means we're invested in making sure you succeed.
How does the 7-day payout work?+
Once your sales are confirmed, your commission is processed and paid to you within 7 calendar days. No chasing, no delays. We take our payment commitment seriously.
Can I do this alongside my current job?+
Absolutely. Many of our best agents started part-time. There are no set hours — you work when you want, as much or as little as you choose. BFS fits around your life.
Is there a limit to how much I can earn?+
No limit whatsoever. Commission scales with what you sell. There's no salary ceiling, no cap, no quota to hit. Your income is entirely in your own hands.
